# Edit Article

Edit and improve articles through a two-step process: section restructuring followed by section-by-section rewriting.

## Process

### 1. Section Organization

Divide the article into sections based on its headings. Think about the main points to be made during each section.

Consider that information is a directed acyclic graph — pieces of information can depend on other pieces of information. Ensure the order of sections and their contents respects these dependencies. No section should reference concepts that haven't been introduced yet.

Present the proposed section structure to the user and confirm before proceeding.

### 2. Section Rewriting

For each section, rewrite to improve clarity, coherence, and flow. Keep paragraphs focused on a single idea and prefer shorter paragraphs for readability.
